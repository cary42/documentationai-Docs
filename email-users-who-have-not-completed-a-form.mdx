## Email users by form completion status

Target users who have not completed a specific form by sending an email directly from that form's settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the form" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    - Go to **Forms** from the main navigation.

    - Locate the form you want to follow up on in the list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open form email settings" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    - Next to the form name, find the **settings icon** on the left side.

    - Click the **settings icon** to open the **Form Settings** menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start the email" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    - In the **Form Settings** menu, select **Send Email**.

    - The email options for that form open so you can choose who should receive the message.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose which users to email" icon="users" title-type="p">
    - Select one or more completion statuses to target:

      - **Not Started**

      - **In Progress**

      - **Pending Approval**

      - **Approved**

    - Campium sends the email only to users whose status on this form matches the options you select.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View names under each form status

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Use the status icons on the **Form Settings** page to see exactly which users fall under each completion status before you send your email.
</Callout>

On the **Form Settings** page for your form, look to the top right where you see the counts for **In Progress**, **Pending Approval**, and **Approved**.\
Click the small icons next to each count to view the list of people with that status for the form.

Use these lists to confirm who will receive your email when you target specific statuses.

## Notes

- Status filters apply only to the selected form, so a user might have a different status on another form.

- The **settings icon** you need is immediately to the left of the form name in the Forms list.

- Reviewing the status icons and counts in the top-right of the Form Settings page helps you confirm your recipient group before sending.
