---
title: Do staff have access to high resolution downloads?
description: >-
  Staff can view more gallery photos than families, but they must still purchase
  the high resolution package to download.
---
## Answer

**Staff can see more photos than families** when they sign in through the parent portal or mobile app.

Staff can browse photos from **all active seasons**, not only the seasons connected to their own family. They also do not need a registration in order to view those galleries.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  **High-resolution download rules stay the same for staff and families.** Unlimited full-resolution downloads still require the one-time season photo purchase, unless that access was given to the staff member another way.
</Callout>

## What this means in practice

Staff get broader access for **viewing** photos, but that does not automatically give them broader access for **downloading** full-resolution files.

If a staff member has not been given special download access another way, they follow the same rule as families and need the season photo purchase for unlimited full-resolution downloads.
