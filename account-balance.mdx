## Overview

Use the **Account Balance** report (Accounts Receivable) to see which families still owe money for a selected season or program group and how those balances compare to their scheduled payments.

The report lists all families with a non-zero balance for the selected season, along with contact details, registration information, charges, scheduled payments, and current balance.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Use this report when you want a collections view by family: who owes money, how much they owe, how much is already scheduled to be paid, and where those numbers do not line up.
</Callout>

## What the report shows

The Account Balance report is scoped by **season/program group**. It includes only families with a **non-zero balance** for the selected season or program group(s).

Each row represents a **family account** and includes:

- Key identifiers (family name and account number)

- Registration context (plan, registration date, campers, products)

- Contact information

- Financial totals (charges, scheduled payments, and outstanding balance)

Use it alongside your scheduled payments and deposit reports to monitor whether what is owed is actually planned to be collected.

## Filters

### Season / program group

The primary filter is **Season / program group**.

- **Season / program group**: Select the season or program group you want to review. The report then shows families with non-zero balances for that selection.

If you use multiple program groups within a season, run the report once per group if you want to focus on a specific camp or program.

## Columns in the Account Balance report

This section describes the main columns you will see for each family row.

### Family and registration details

- **Name**: The family account name.

- **Account Number**: The internal account identifier for the family.

- **Plan**: The payment plan or arrangement associated with the family’s registration for the selected season or program group.

- **Registration Date**: The date the family completed registration for that season or program group.

- **Campers**: The campers linked to this family’s registration for the selected season.

- **Products**: The camp sessions, add-ons, or other products tied to the family’s registration.

### Contact information

- **Primary contact info**: The primary contact details for the family, typically including email and phone, so you can reach out about outstanding balances or payment plan changes.

### Financial totals

- **Charges**: The total charges for this family for the selected season or program group. This is what the family owes before payments.

- **Scheduled**: The total amount of **future scheduled payments** for this family that have not yet been completed for the relevant charges.

- **Balance**: The current outstanding balance for the family for the selected season or program group, after payments and credits.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Use **Charges** vs **Balance** to see how much of the family’s total fees remain unpaid, and compare **Scheduled** vs **Balance** to confirm whether future scheduled payments will fully cover what is still owed.
</Callout>

## Red-row mismatch warning

<Callout kind="alert" collapsed="false">
  Rows highlighted in red indicate a **mismatch** between the **Balance** and **Scheduled** amounts. In these rows, the total scheduled future payments do **not** equal the outstanding balance for that family.
</Callout>

When you see a red row:

- Review the family’s scheduled payments and payment plan.

- Confirm whether additional payments need to be scheduled, or if charges, discounts, or manual payments need adjustment.

- Follow up with the family if the plan does not fully cover the remaining balance.

Red-highlighted families are your priority list for fixing gaps between what is owed and what is currently planned to be collected.

## Run the report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Financial reports menu" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    - Sign in to your Campium admin portal.

    - Open the **Financials** navigation.

    - Select **Accounts Receivable**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the season / program group" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    - In the **Season / program group** filter, select the season or program group you want to review.

    - If needed, repeat the report for other program groups within the same season.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to use this report in your workflow

Use the Account Balance report regularly during your season to:

- Identify families with outstanding balances that are **not fully covered** by scheduled payments (red rows).

- Confirm that families on plans have enough future payments scheduled to cover their balances.

- Prioritize outreach to families who need updated payment plans or additional payments before session start dates.
