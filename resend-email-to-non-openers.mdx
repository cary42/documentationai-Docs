## Resend a sent email to non-openers

Resend a mass email from Communications to only the recipients who have not opened the original message.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Non-openers are recipients who received the original email but have not opened it yet based on Campium's email tracking.
</Callout>

### Steps to resend to non-openers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your sent email" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    - Go to **Communications → Emails** or **Communications → Mass Emails**.

    - Find the sent email you want to resend in the list and click to open its details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use the non-openers link" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    - Under the **Messages Sent** count, look for the yellow link that reads **Resend to X non-openers** (for example, `Resend to 5 non-openers`).

    - Click this link and confirm that you want to send the email again to all non-openers.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-0d8eec32-96fa-419f-9505-03b1e136b066/doc-76bb5730-4569-4c2d-926d-aa8a7b125b49/1775501082537-ub8deku9y9m-download--5-.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="367" height="184" alt="" style="width: 367px; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delivery after confirmation" icon="play-circle" title-type="p">
    - After you confirm, Campium queues the resend emails.

    - The messages are sent shortly to all recipients who have not yet opened the original email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Notes

- The **Resend to X non-openers** link appears directly under the **Messages Sent** total on the email detail screen.

- The number in the link text (`X`) matches how many recipients have not opened the email yet.

- The resend goes to **all** current non-openers for that email; you do not need to build a separate list.
