---
title: How do I skip the deposit?
---
### **Steps**

1. Open **Admin -> Discounts**

2. When creating or editing a discount find the setting **Bypass payment at portal checkout**.

3. Set it to **Yes**.

4. Save changes.

### **What this does**

- Portal checkout allows registration without immediate deposit/payment but ensures the family puts an account on file.

- Families can finish checkout now, payment can be collected later.

### **Quick test**

1. Open parent portal flow for your sit

2. Start test registration.

3. Confirm checkout no longer requires deposit to complete.

### **If it still asks for deposit**

- Confirm setting saved in correct account/site.

- Recheck **Bypass payment at portal checkout = Yes**.

- Check other payment rules/required fees in the same settings area.

- Retry in private/incognito window to avoid cached session values.
