## Health Report

The Health Report is a printable PDF that puts one person’s health details in one place. Use it when you need a paper copy or a single file to review, share, or keep on hand.

It can include:

- Basic profile details

- Guardian details

- Health questions you choose

- Insurance details

- Medications

## Open the Health Report from a profile

Use the person’s profile to download the latest version of the report.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the person’s profile" title-type="p">
    Go to the person’s profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the report" title-type="p">
    Select **Actions**, then choose **Download health report (PDF)**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make a field show on the report

Turn on the report setting for any profile field you want to include.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open profile fields" title-type="p">
    Go to **Admin** > **Profile Fields**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit the field" title-type="p">
    Find the field you want, then open it to edit.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Include it on the report" title-type="p">
    Turn on **Include on health report**, then click **Update Profile Field**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you are creating a new field, turn on **Include on health report** before you save it.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  The report shows the latest saved version of the field settings. If you just changed a field, download the PDF again to refresh what you see.
</Callout>

## If a field still does not appear

Check the most common causes before you try again.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm you are viewing the right user type" title-type="p">
    Open the field settings and make sure the field is meant for the type of person you are viewing, such as camper or staff.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check whether the answer is filled in" title-type="p">
    If the field has no answer yet, the report may show a blank line instead of a value.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download a new copy" title-type="p">
    Save your changes, then download the PDF again so you are looking at the latest version.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  If the field is still missing after these checks, review the field settings once more and confirm that **Include on health report** is turned on.
</Callout>
