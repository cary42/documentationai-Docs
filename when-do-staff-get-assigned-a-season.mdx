## Overview

Staff Season on a staff profile controls season-based reporting and tracking. Use this page to understand exactly when a staff member is added to a season or removed from one based on their form responses and approval status.

## When staff are added to a season

- They finish a hiring form you marked `Staff season assignment = Yes`. The season is added once the form is fully submitted and marked **Pending** or **Approved**, whether it is a standard staff form or a registration staff form for that season.
- You approve their form if it was not already approved when they submitted it. You can approve the form from the profile, form responses, or with bulk approve.
- They have more than one season-assignment form for the same season. Each approved or completed path can add the season, and one approved form is enough to keep them assigned to that season.

## When staff are removed from a season

- You change their form from **Approved** to another status, such as **Rejected**, **Pending approval**, or **In progress**, on a form marked as a season-assignment form.
- You delete their form response if that response was **Approved**.
- They still have another **Approved** season-assignment form for the same season. Removing or changing one form does not remove the season until no approved season-assignment forms remain for that season.

## Form setting

<Callout kind="info">

On **Form Settings**, turn on **Staff season assignment** for your main hiring application for that season. Reference or supplemental forms can leave this setting off.

</Callout>
