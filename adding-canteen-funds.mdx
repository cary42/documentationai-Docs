## Add funds from the admin site

Add money to a camper or staff member's canteen account from the admin site when someone pays in person at the office. Use the **Canteen Deposits** screen to record the deposit and update their balance right away.

## Where to go

1. Sign in to the admin site.
2. Open **Canteen** in the main menu.
3. Select **Deposits**.
4. Click **Add Funds** in the page header.

## Who can do this

You need access to **Canteen Deposits** to add money to a user's balance. This access is controlled by the `canteen_purchases` permission.

<Callout kind="info">

If you do not see **Canteen** or **Deposits**, ask an administrator to check your role permissions. Your account also needs the canteen module enabled before these screens appear.

</Callout>

## Step-by-step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Funds" icon="plus">

  From **Canteen Deposits**, click **Add Funds**. The **Add funds** modal opens.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the user" icon="users">

  In **User**, start typing the person's name in the search box, then select them from the list. Their current canteen balance appears below the user field.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the payment account" icon="settings">

  In **Account**, choose how the payment was received:

  - **Cash** if **Accept cash** is turned on in Canteen settings
  - **Zelle**
  - A saved **card** or **bank account** on the family's file

  Saved card and bank account options load after you select the user.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount" icon="edit">

  Enter the deposit amount. As you type, the modal updates both **Current balance** and **New balance** so you can confirm the change before saving.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the deposit" icon="check-circle">

  Click **Add funds** to save the deposit. Click **Cancel** to close the modal without saving.

  If your camp charges a credit card surcharge, the modal shows a note at the top with the surcharge percentage.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after

- The deposit is recorded on **Canteen Deposits**.
- The user's canteen balance increases and can be used for **User Account** purchases at the canteen register.
- Confirm the deposit on **Canteen Deposits** or check the balance on **Canteen Account Balances**.

## Related screens

| Screen | Use for |
| --- | --- |
| Canteen Deposits | Add funds and review deposit history |
| Canteen Account Balances | See who has money left; View activity for deposit vs. purchase breakdown |
| Canteen (main POS) | Ring up purchases charged to a user's balance, not for adding funds |

## Families adding funds online

Families can also add canteen funds in the parent portal from **Canteen** in the menu or from **Account**, using a saved payment method. Office staff should use the admin **Add Funds** flow above when payment is taken in person.

<Callout kind="info">

The parent portal minimum deposit is typically **$3**.

</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely cause |
| --- | --- |
| No Canteen menu | Module off or your login lacks canteen permissions |
| No Cash in Account dropdown | Accept cash is off in Canteen settings |
| No cards or bank accounts listed | Family has nothing on file; use Cash or Zelle, or have them add a payment method first |
| Add funds fails | Note the user, amount, and time; check the error in the modal and retry once, then escalate |
| Balance looks wrong | On Account Balances, use View activity. Balance equals deposits minus purchases, with deposits shown net of fees when applicable |
