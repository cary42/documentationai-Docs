## Manage staff access from one page

**CampiumDB Users** shows every person in your organization who has a staff-facing role, such as office staff, nurses, or administrators. Use this page to see who can sign in, assign roles, change statuses, and manage access across organization accounts.

Parents and campers do not appear here. This page only lists people who can sign in to the admin side of CampiumDB.

## Access and permissions

CampiumDB Users lives in the Admin section of Campium.

<Callout kind="info">

To access **CampiumDB Users**, your staff role must include the **Create Role** permission. If you do not see **CampiumDB Users** under **Admin**, ask an Administrator to grant access or make the change for you.

</Callout>

## Open CampiumDB Users

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Admin menu" icon="settings" title-type="p">

  - Sign in to CampiumDB.
  - Open the left navigation.
  - Expand **Admin**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Open CampiumDB Users" icon="users" title-type="p">

  - Click **CampiumDB Users**.
  - The page opens with a table of staff users who have a role in the current organization account.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## What appears on the page

The main table lists staff members who already have a role. Each row shows what access they have in the account you are currently using.

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | The person's name. Click it to open their full profile. |
| **Role** | The role assigned in the current organization account. Click it to change the role. |
| **Status** | The user's current status, such as Active or Inactive. Click it to change the status. |
| **Last Login** | The last time the person signed in. A blank value means they have never signed in. |
| **Access** | A button for managing access to other organization accounts. This appears only if your organization has more than one account. |

The top right of the page includes **Add New User With Role**.

## Add a new user with a role

Use this option when the person already exists in CampiumDB as a staff record and now needs a login with a role.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the new user flow" icon="plus" title-type="p">

  - Click **Add New User With Role**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose staff members" icon="users" title-type="p">

  - In **Staff Names**, start typing the person's name.
  - Select the person from the list.
  - Add more than one person if needed. Everyone selected in this step receives the same role.
  - Only staff members with an **Active** staff status appear in the search.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign the role" icon="edit" title-type="p">

  - In **Role to Assign**, choose the role to give them, such as **Administrator**, **Office**, or **Camp Nurse**.
  - Roles come from **Admin → Roles & Permissions**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the change" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">

  - Click **Save**.
  - The page reloads and the new user appears in the list.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

If the person does not appear in **Staff Names**, either they do not have a staff record yet or their staff status is not Active. Add them as a staff member or reactivate them from their profile, then return to **CampiumDB Users**.

</Callout>

## Change someone's role

A role controls what the person can do after they sign in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the role editor" icon="edit" title-type="p">

  - Find the person in the list.
  - Click their **Role**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the new role" icon="users" title-type="p">

  - Choose the new role from the **Role** dropdown.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the role" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">

  - Click **Save Role**.

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Remove a role

If you select a blank role, CampiumDB sets the person's status to **Inactive** so they can no longer sign in.

<Callout kind="info">

If you want to remove the role without changing the person's current status, select **Keep the user status the same** before saving. This matters when the person should remain active in another user type, such as an active camper or parent.

</Callout>

## Change someone's status

Status controls whether the person can sign in and how they appear in reports. Use **Inactive** when someone leaves and should no longer have access.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the status editor" icon="edit" title-type="p">

  - Click the **Status** link in the person's row.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the new status" icon="settings" title-type="p">

  - Select the new **Status**, such as **Active** or **Inactive**.
  - Optionally choose one or more **User Types**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the update" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">

  - Click **Change Status**.

  </Step>
</Steps>

Keeping a user inactive preserves their profile and history. You do not need to delete the user to stop sign-in access.

## Open the full profile

Click the person's **Name** in the list to open their full profile. The profile contains contact information, custom fields, history, and other user details.

## Manage account access across organization accounts

If your organization uses more than one CampiumDB organization account, use **Account access** to let a staff member open another account and work there with a specific role.

This setting does not change the person's role in the account you are currently viewing. To change their role in the current account, use the **Role** link on the **CampiumDB Users** page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account access" icon="users" title-type="p">

  - Click **Account access** on the person's row.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the other account" icon="plus" title-type="p">

  - Add a row for each other organization account they should be able to open.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the role for that account" icon="edit" title-type="p">

  - Select the **Role** they should have in each additional account.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Save account access" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">

  - Click **Save**.

  </Step>
</Steps>

For full instructions, see [Account access across organization accounts](/staff-organization-account-access).

## Common situations

### A new staff member should be able to sign in as an Administrator

Add them as a staff member first. Then use **Add New User With Role**, choose their name, assign **Administrator**, and save.

### Someone is leaving and should no longer be able to sign in

Open their **Status**, change it to **Inactive**, and save. Their login stops working immediately, while their history remains in place.

### Someone needs a different level of access

Open their **Role**, choose the new role, and save. Use this for promotions, reduced access, or changes in job responsibilities.

### Someone works in two organization accounts

Set their role in the current account on **CampiumDB Users**, then use **Account access** to assign a role in the other account.

### A person should be here but does not appear

CampiumDB Users only shows people who already have a role. If they are a staff member without a role, add them with **Add New User With Role**. Parents and campers never appear in this list.

## Frequently asked questions

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What is the difference between status and role?" default-open="false">

  **Role** controls what the person can do after signing in, including which menus and tools they can use. **Status** controls whether they can sign in at all.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="If I remove someone's role, is their account deleted?" default-open="false">

  No. CampiumDB does not delete the account. It sets the person to **Inactive** so they can no longer sign in, while keeping their profile, history, and data in place.

  If you do not want the status to change when removing the role, select **Keep the user status the same** before saving.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Why does the role dropdown not show the role I expect?" default-open="false">

  Roles are managed under **Admin → Roles & Permissions**. If the role is missing, create it there and then return to **CampiumDB Users**.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I add more than one person at once?" default-open="false">

  Yes. The **Staff Names** field lets you select multiple people during **Add New User With Role**. Everyone selected receives the same role.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What does Last Login show?" default-open="false">

  **Last Login** shows the last time the person signed in to CampiumDB. A blank value means the account exists but has never been used to sign in.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Who can use this page?" default-open="false">

  Staff members whose role includes the **Create Role** permission can use **CampiumDB Users**. This permission is typically assigned to Administrators.

  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
