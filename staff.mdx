## Set up staff applications in the same portal families use

Staff apply through your normal portal website, not a separate staff-only site. After applicants log in as **Staff**, they see forms and tasks meant for staff, while your team uses CampiumDB to build forms, review applications, and manage hiring.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Campium has two login destinations:

  - **CampiumDB** is your admin site for directors, HR, and office staff.

  - **Your portal** is the website applicants and families use.

  Send staff applicants to the same portal URL families use, such as `https://yourcamp.campium.com`.
</Callout>

## Build your staff application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the form" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    In CampiumDB, go to **Communication → Forms** and add a new form. If you already have a staff application from a previous season, copying it is often the fastest starting point.

    Choose the form type that matches your hiring flow:

    - **Standard (No Products)** — best for most staff applications that only collect answers.

    - **Registration (With Products)** — use this only when applicants must choose sessions or pay a fee as part of applying.

    - **Upload a File** — use this when you only need a document upload.

    When this step is complete, you have a form shell ready for recipients, settings, and questions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Target the form to staff" icon="users" title-type="p">
    Open the form and go to the **Recipients** tab. Under **User types**, select **Staff**.

    Then choose who should receive the form:

    - **All Active/Inactive/Pending staff** — the usual choice for open hiring.

    - **Only people who match the filters below** — use this when you want to limit access to a specific group, such as invited applicants.

    <Callout kind="alert" collapsed="false">
      If **Staff** is not selected on the Recipients tab, the form will not appear for staff on the portal.
    </Callout>

    Save your changes before moving on.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add application questions as you normally would" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Go to the **Questions** tab and add the information you need to evaluate applicants.

    Common staff application questions include:

    - Experience working with children

    - Certifications and licenses

    - Availability

    - References

    - Position preferences

    - Housing or scheduling needs

    A completed Questions tab gives applicants everything they need to submit one clear application.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect the form to Staff Hub" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    Go to **Admin → Staff Hub** and open **Staff Hub Settings**. Under **Staff form by season**, select the staff application form for each season you are hiring for.

    You can also choose which profile fields appear as columns in the Staff Hub list. Save after you finish so your hiring team can track applicants in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How staff apply

Staff usually apply in one of two ways. The best option depends on whether applicants create their own accounts or your team creates accounts first.

### Path A: Applicants create their own account

On the portal login page, applicants go to **Create Your Account**, choose **Staff** from the **I am** dropdown, and then log in to complete the application.

You can change the label applicants see for that dropdown choice under **Portal → Settings**. Once they are logged in, the staff application appears if the form is visible and the Recipients rules include them.

### Path B: Your team creates the account first

Create the staff person in CampiumDB before they apply by going to **People → New Person**. Then send them to the portal to log in with that account and complete the staff application.

This path works well when you want tighter control over who can apply. The form still only appears if the person matches the Recipients settings on the form.

## What staff can do on the portal

After staff log in to the portal, they see the items you have enabled and targeted to them.

| Portal feature     | What staff can do                                                             |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Forms**          | Complete the staff application and any other forms sent to Staff.             |
| **Contracts**      | Review and sign employment paperwork you sent them.                           |
| **Clock in / out** | Punch in and out for the day if staff time tracking is enabled on the portal. |

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Staff do not see camper registration forms unless you also targeted those forms to **Staff**.
</Callout>

## Manage applicants and staff records in CampiumDB

CampiumDB is where your team reviews applications, updates status, and gives hired staff any internal access they need.

### Staff Hub

Go to **Admin → Staff Hub** to track everyone who started your staff application for a season. Staff Hub helps your team work from one hiring list instead of opening each profile one by one.

Use Staff Hub to:

- Update application statuses such as **Hired**, **Interview**, or **Declined**

- Edit profile fields from the list

- See which forms each person started or finished

- Review contract status, including whether a contract was sent or signed

- Send contracts from the list

### Staff profiles

Open a staff member's profile to view their full record, including **Staff Season**. This is the quickest place to confirm which seasons they applied for or worked.

If a form is missing for a specific person, run **Check Permissions** on their profile. Check Permissions shows why a form does or does not appear for that staff member.

### Contracts

If Contracts is enabled in **Site Settings**, your team can build contract templates that fill in staff information automatically. Send contracts from Staff Hub or from the Contracts area, and staff complete the signature step on the portal.

When a contract is signed, your team is notified. This keeps application review and paperwork completion in the same workflow.

### Staff Scheduler

If Staff Scheduler is enabled, build weekly or monthly schedules, assign shifts, and publish them for staff to view. You can also email staff when a schedule is updated.

This is separate from applying, but it becomes part of the same staff record after hiring.

### Time tracking

Campium supports two staff time tracking workflows:

| Setting                              | How it works                                                        |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Staff Time Tracking On CampiumDB** | Your team clocks staff in and out from the admin attendance screen. |
| **Staff Time Tracking On Portal**    | Staff clock themselves in and out on the portal.                    |

### Give hired staff access to CampiumDB

Applying on the portal does not give someone access to CampiumDB. CampiumDB is your internal admin system.

To give a hired staff member admin access, go to **Admin → CampiumDB Users** and click **Add New User With Role**. Find the staff member's name and assign the appropriate role, such as **Office**, **Nurse**, or **Administrator**.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Parents and campers never appear in **CampiumDB Users**. Only people with an assigned admin role can log in to CampiumDB.
</Callout>

## Quick checklist

1. Create the form, usually as **Standard (No Products)**.

2. On **Recipients**, set **User types** to **Staff**.

3. Set the season, start and end dates, and switch visibility to **Visible** when ready.

4. Set **Staff season assignment** to **Yes** on the main hiring form only.

5. Add your application questions.

6. Map the form in **Staff Hub Settings**.

7. Turn on portal registration if applicants will create their own accounts.

8. Test the process by logging in as Staff or by using Test Mode while building.

9. Use Staff Hub to track submissions and update statuses.

## Common questions

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Where is the staff portal?" default-open="false">
    There is no separate staff portal. Send applicants to the same portal website families use, and have them log in as **Staff**.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="The form does not show for someone" default-open="false">
    Check each of these items:

    - The form's **Recipients** tab includes **Staff**

    - The applicant matches any filters you set

    - The form is **Visible**

    - Today's date is between the form's start and end dates

    - The account is set up as **Staff**

    - **Check Permissions** on the person's profile confirms they can see the form
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What is the difference between the main application and extra forms?" default-open="false">
    Use **Staff season assignment = Yes** on the main application only. That form represents applying for the season and adds the season to the staff profile.

    Use separate forms for references, background checks, handbooks, and similar follow-up tasks. On those forms, keep **Staff** on the Recipients tab and set **Staff season assignment = No**.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can staff see CampiumDB?" default-open="false">
    Staff can only access CampiumDB if you add them in **CampiumDB Users** and assign a role. Completing a staff application on the portal does not create admin access.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
