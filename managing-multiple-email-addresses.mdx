## Managing multiple email addresses

Last Updated: **September 10, 2024**

Users can have multiple email addresses associated with a single Campium account so families and staff do not need separate, duplicate profiles for each email.

<Callout kind="tip">

Using multiple email addresses on one profile reduces duplicate accounts and keeps a user's information, registrations, and payments in one place.

</Callout>

## Add additional email addresses to a user

Use the Manage Emails button on a user's profile to add secondary addresses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the user's profile" icon="users" title-type="p">

  - Go to the relevant user's profile in Campium.
  - Confirm you are on the correct person or family before changing email addresses.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Manage Emails" icon="edit" title-type="p">

  - On the profile, click the blue **Manage Emails** button.
  - Review the **primary email address**, which appears first in the list.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add additional email addresses and save" icon="plus" title-type="p">

  - Enter each additional email address you want associated with this account.
  - After adding or editing addresses, click **Save** to apply your updates.

  </Step>
</Steps>

After you save, the new email addresses are linked to the same user account.

<Callout kind="info">

Whenever you update the email addresses on a profile, Campium sends a verification email to the user so they can confirm the changes.

</Callout>

## Remove an email address from a user

Remove an email if it no longer belongs to the user or should not receive communication.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Manage Emails on the profile" icon="users" title-type="p">

  - Go to the user's profile.
  - Click the blue **Manage Emails** button to see all email addresses on the account.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove the email address" icon="trash" title-type="p">

  - Remove the email address you no longer want associated with the user.
  - The email address is removed from the system immediately once you complete this action.

  </Step>
</Steps>

The removed email can no longer be used for that account or receive communication tied to this profile.
