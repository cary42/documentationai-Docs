## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Basic camp details ready (name, dates, location)
- Admin access permissions if managing a team account
</Callout>

Campium streamlines camp operations like registration and attendance. Follow these steps to get up and running quickly.

## Create and Verify Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes to access your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.campium.com` and click **Sign Up**.

    Enter your email, create a password, and provide your camp name (e.g., "Sunny Valley Summer Camp").

    Agree to the terms and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Campium.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your credentials.

    You'll land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Basic Camp Settings

Set up your camp profile to enable registrations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings" icon="settings">
    From the dashboard, click **Settings** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Camp Details" icon="edit-3">
    Fill in:
    
    | Field          | Example Value                  |
    |----------------|--------------------------------|
    | Camp Name      | Sunny Valley Summer Camp       |
    | Session Dates  | June 15 - August 15, 2024     |
    | Location       | 123 Camp Road, Anywhere, CA    |
    | Max Capacity   | 150 campers                    |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Click **Save Settings**. A confirmation banner appears.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Admin Dashboard

The dashboard organizes key tools. Use these sections to manage daily operations.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Registrations" icon="user-check">
    View incoming registrations, approve campers, and track payments.

    Key actions:
    - Search by name or email
    - Export roster as PDF
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Attendance" icon="check-circle">
    Mark daily check-ins, track absences, and generate reports.

    Features include mobile check-in for staff.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Billing" icon="dollar-sign">
    Manage invoices, process payments, and set up recurring billing.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Add Your First Camper Registration

Test the system with a sample registration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Registration" icon="plus">
    Go to **Registrations > New Registration**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Camper Details" icon="user">
    Enter sample data:

    - Name: Jane Doe
    - Age: 10
    - Allergies: None
    - Emergency Contact: John Doe, 555-1234
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Submit" icon="check">
    Preview the form, then click **Submit Registration**.

    View it in the registrations list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Congratulations! You've completed your first camper registration. Explore reports next to analyze data.
</Callout>
