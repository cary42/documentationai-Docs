## Configure Basic Camp Settings

Set up your camp profile to enable registrations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    From the dashboard, click **Admin -> Site Settings** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save" title-type="p">
    Click **Save Settings**. A confirmation banner appears.
  </Step>
</Steps>
