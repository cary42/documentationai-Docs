---
title: Cannot Access Project Workspace
description: Fix issues when you can't access a workspace in Campium.
---

## Common Symptoms

- Workspace not visible
- Access denied error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    Sometimes sessions expire. Log out, then log back in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check workspace permissions">
    Ask the workspace owner if your account has access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Campium support">
    Email support@campium.com if the issue persists.
  </Step>
</Steps>
