---
title: Organize Documentation
description: How to structure your docs in Campium.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click **New Project** from any workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add categories">
    Use the **Add Category** option to group related docs (e.g., Guides, API).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload documents">
    Click **Add Document** or drag & drop files to upload your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rearrange documents">
    Drag docs or categories to change their order in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>
