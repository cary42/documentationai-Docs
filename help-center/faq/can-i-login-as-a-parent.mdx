## Log in as a parent from their family profile

Use the parent impersonation link in a family profile to see exactly what that parent sees in the parent website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the family profile" icon="users" title-type="p">
    - Log in to your Campium staff account.

    - Search for and open the family profile that includes the parent.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the parent profile" icon="user" title-type="p">
    - In the family, click the specific parent's name to open their profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use the login link" icon="log-in" title-type="p">
    - Look below the parent's photo for the **Login&#x20;**&#x62;utton and click it.

    - If you do not see it there, make sure you're looking at the parent or staff profile. You can not log in as a child.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-0d8eec32-96fa-419f-9505-03b1e136b066/doc-76bb5730-4569-4c2d-926d-aa8a7b125b49/1775477519712-dmgmeajzhms-pasted-image-1775477511075.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1089" height="537" alt="" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the parent site" icon="monitor" title-type="p">
    - After you click the link, Campium opens the parent website portal as if you were that parent.

    - Review or troubleshoot the account as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Always log out of the parent site when you are finished impersonating so you do not accidentally perform actions as that parent later.
</Callout>
