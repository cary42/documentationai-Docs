## Allow staff to email only their assigned families

Set up sessions so staff and counselors can log in to CampiumDB and email only the families they are responsible for.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Attach staff to their sessions" icon="users" title-type="p">
    - Go to the **Seasons** section in your CampiumDB admin view.

    - Edit the session where the staff member or counselor should work.

    - In the session settings, add the correct **Staff** so they are attached to that session.

    - Save your changes.

    When you finish, each staff member is explicitly linked to the sessions they oversee.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-0d8eec32-96fa-419f-9505-03b1e136b066/doc-76bb5730-4569-4c2d-926d-aa8a7b125b49/1775478294410-71bp7t08dco-pasted-image-1775478293093.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="733" height="696" alt="" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Have staff log in and open their sessions" icon="monitor" title-type="p">
    - Ask the staff member to log in to **CampiumDB** with their account.

    - On their dashboard, they will see a list of the **sessions** they are assigned to.

    This list is filtered to only the sessions where you attached them as staff.



    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-0d8eec32-96fa-419f-9505-03b1e136b066/doc-76bb5730-4569-4c2d-926d-aa8a7b125b49/1775478319382-tc6ti9sf88k-CampiumDB-Dashboard-04-06-2026_08_14_AM.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1335" height="490" alt="" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Email all families in a session" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    - From the staff dashboard, click a session in the list.

    - In the session row, click the **people icon** on the left to open the list of children.

    - Review the children and the **email addresses** associated with their families.

    - Click **Send Email** to email all those families at once.

    - (Optional) Add **file attachments** to the email before sending.

    Staff messages go only to the families linked to that session's children.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting and visuals

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  If a staff member does not see any sessions on their dashboard, go back to **Seasons**, edit the relevant session, and confirm they are added under **Staff** for that session.
</Callout>
