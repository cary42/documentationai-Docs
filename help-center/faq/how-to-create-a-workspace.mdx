---
title: How do I create a workspace?
description: Learn how to start a workspace in Campium.
---

## Steps

1. Log in to your Campium account.
2. Click the **"New Workspace"** button on your dashboard.
3. Enter a workspace name and optional description.
4. Click **Create** to finish.

Your workspace will appear on the dashboard for organizing documentation.

## Tip

You can invite team members anytime from the workspace settings.
